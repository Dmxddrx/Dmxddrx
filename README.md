## About Me
I am an **Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering student** at the **Institute of Technology, University of Moratuwa**, with a strong passion for designing and building **practical electronic systems** that solve real-world problems.

My interests span across **embedded systems, hardware engineering, PCB design, and IoT architecture**. I enjoy working close to the hardware layer—bringing ideas to life from schematics to functioning prototypes.

Alongside engineering, I have experience in **technical documentation, presentations, and graphic design**, and a solid foundation in programming that I continuously expand through hands-on projects.
